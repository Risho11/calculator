About this calculator

Three discount calculations depending on what you know. Use the first tab when shopping. Use the second tab to compare sale prices. Use the third tab when you see a sale price tagged but want to know the original.

Common uses

  • Verifying the final price of a sale item
  • Comparing discounts across different stores
  • Back-calculating original prices from clearance tags
  • Calculating coupon savings

FAQ

Does this include tax?
No. The calculator works on the price before tax. Add tax separately after you have the discounted price.
Can I use it for multiple discounts stacked?
Not directly in one step. For stacked discounts (e.g. 20% off then an extra 10% off), run two calculations sequentially.
