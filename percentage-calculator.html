About this calculator

Four common percentage calculations in one place. Switch between tabs depending on what you know and what you need to find.

Which tab to use

  • X% of Y — "What is 20% of 150?" → 30
  • X is what % of Y — "30 is what percent of 150?" → 20%
  • % Change — "What is the % change from 80 to 100?" → 25% increase
  • Add / Remove % — "Add 15% to 200" → 230, or "Remove 15% from 200" → 170

FAQ

How do I find what percent one number is of another?
Use the "What percent is X of Y?" section. Enter the two values and the calculator shows the percentage instantly.
Can I calculate percentage increase or decrease?
Yes — the Percentage Change section calculates increase or decrease between two values as a percentage.
How do I add or remove a percentage from a value?
Use the "Add/Remove percent" section. Enter the value and the percent to add or subtract.
