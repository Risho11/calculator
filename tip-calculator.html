About this calculator

Enter your bill, choose a tip percentage (or use a quick preset), and set how many people are splitting the bill. The result shows the tip amount, total bill, and per-person amounts.

The calculator updates automatically when you change any value.

Tipping guidelines (US)

  • 10% — Minimum for poor or takeout service
  • 15% — Standard for acceptable service
  • 18% — Good service
  • 20% — Very good service (common default)
  • 25%+ — Exceptional service

Tipping norms vary by country. These reflect US conventions.

FAQ

Should tip be calculated on the pre-tax or post-tax amount?
Convention varies. Most people tip on the pre-tax total; some tip on the post-tax total. Enter whichever number you prefer.
Can I enter a custom tip percentage?
Yes — just type it into the Tip % field directly.
