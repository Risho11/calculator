About Calculator Prodigy

Calculator Prodigy is a free collection of online calculators. All calculations run in your browser — no account needed, no data sent anywhere.

What's here

Ten calculators covering everyday math needs: a basic four-function calculator, a scientific calculator with trig and logarithms, percentage calculations in four forms, age from birthdate, BMI from height and weight, tip and bill splitting, unit conversion across 8 categories, date arithmetic, loan payment estimates, and discount calculations in three directions.

Accuracy

Results are provided for general informational use. For financial or medical decisions, verify results with a qualified professional. The loan calculator uses standard amortization formulas; the BMI calculator uses WHO standard formulas.
