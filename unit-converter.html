About this converter

Select a category, enter your value, choose your source and target units, and click Convert. Results update automatically when you change units. Conversion results are shown to 8 significant figures.

Supported categories

  • Length — meters, km, miles, yards, feet, inches, nautical miles, and more
  • Weight — kg, grams, pounds, ounces, tons, stone
  • Temperature — Celsius, Fahrenheit, Kelvin
  • Volume — liters, ml, US gallons, cups, fluid oz, cubic meters
  • Area — square meters, km², ft², acres, hectares
  • Speed — m/s, km/h, mph, knots
  • Time — seconds, minutes, hours, days, weeks, months, years
  • Data — bytes, KB, MB, GB, TB, bits, kilobits, megabits

FAQ

What unit categories are supported?
Length, weight, temperature, volume, area, speed, time, and data/storage units.
Can I convert between metric and imperial?
Yes — for example, kilometers to miles, kilograms to pounds, or Celsius to Fahrenheit.
Is temperature conversion included?
Yes — Celsius, Fahrenheit, and Kelvin conversions are all supported.
