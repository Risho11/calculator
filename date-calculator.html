About this calculator

Three date tools in one. Days Between Dates gives the total gap in days, weeks, months, and years. Add/Subtract Days finds a new date by adding or removing a number of days. Day of the Week tells you what day any date falls on.

Common uses

  • How many days until a deadline, event, or vacation
  • What date is 90 days from today?
  • What day of the week is a specific date?
  • How many days between two contract dates

FAQ

What can this date calculator do?
Three things: find the number of days between two dates, add or subtract days from a date, and find what day of the week any date falls on.
Does it account for leap years?
Yes — all calculations handle leap years correctly.
Can I calculate business days?
The calculator returns total calendar days. Business day filtering is not yet supported.
